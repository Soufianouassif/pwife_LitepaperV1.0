PEPEWIFE (PWIFE)

Pepe created the meme, but she builds the future.

1) Executive Summary

PEPEWIFE (PWIFE) is a Solana-based meme token designed to build a long-term, community-driven movement inspired by internet culture, with a disciplined launch approach focused on transparency and responsible liquidity management. The project follows a gradual evolution model: it launches with a clear early utility through a Tap-to-Earn application, then expands over time with additional ecosystem features such as staking and broader community initiatives, supported by vesting schedules designed to reduce sudden sell pressure and strengthen trust.

2) Background & Narrative

Meme coins have evolved from simple internet jokes into global digital communities capable of influencing culture and markets. However, the sector has also gained a negative reputation due to short-lived projects driven primarily by hype, with limited transparency and weak execution plans.

PEPEWIFE presents a different narrative: a community-first meme project that balances culture and creativity with clear commitments around launch discipline, token distribution, and transparency.

3) Vision & Objectives

Vision: Build a community-driven meme token that evolves over time rather than relying solely on short-term speculation.

Objectives:

  • Build a strong, global community powered by participation and creativity.
  • Launch on Solana with clear transparency commitments and responsible liquidity practices.
  • Deliver early utility (Tap-to-Earn) and expand later into staking and additional ecosystem initiatives.
  • Support sustainable growth through gradual token releases (vesting) to reduce market shocks.

4) Why Solana

Solana was chosen because it offers:

  • Low fees suitable for frequent community interactions and micro-rewards.
  • High throughput and fast confirmation times.
  • A strong DeFi and DEX ecosystem (e.g., Raydium) for liquidity and accessibility.

5) Core Principles

  • Community First: The project’s strength and growth are driven by its community.
  • Transparency: Clear communication around allocation, vesting, official wallets, and proofs of key actions (locks, authorities).
  • Long-Term Orientation: A focus on sustainable development rather than short-term hype cycles.
  • Culture & Creativity: Turning meme energy into consistent community activity and participation.

8) Vesting & Release Schedule

To strengthen trust, align incentives, and reduce sudden sell pressure, PWIFE allocations follow a gradual release approach:

  • Team (5%): 12-month vesting (released gradually).
  • Private Sale (3%): 1-month vesting (released gradually over one month).
  • Public Sale (17%): 1-month vesting (released gradually over one month).

A detailed vesting schedule (dates and release percentages) will be published prior to launch via official channels.

9) Liquidity & Launch Plan

  • Liquidity Allocation: 30% of the total supply is allocated for launch liquidity.
  • DEX Launch: Liquidity will be added on Solana DEX platforms such as Raydium.
  • Liquidity Lock: 30% of the launch liquidity (LP) will be locked for 12 months (one full year) to support trust and market stability.
  • Lock Proof: The official liquidity lock proof (locker link / transaction proof) will be published immediately after execution.

Note: The specific lock provider/solution will be selected prior to launch and publicly shared.
